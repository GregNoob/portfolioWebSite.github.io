About

Hi!
My name is Caplea George Alexandru and for the last few years, I have dedicated myself to web designing and all things that exist in the world of front-end development. In february 2022 I've enrolled at Link Academy School, the web developemnt section and during 12 months I have been learning both front-end and back-end technologies mainly JavaScript, HTML, CSS, NodeJS and all the concepts of web development, from simple to advanced, as well as popular frameworks such as React, Vue and Angular, CSS frameworks such as bootstrap, tailwind and others. My educational background is different form anything that comes close to programming, nevertheless I have a solid foundation in computer programming concepts, algoruthms, and data structures. Even so, I have gained key values and people skills that will serve me well in my future career as a web developer, I have problem-solving abilities, I am highly creative, I pay attention to detail and I have the ability to work well within a team. This also gives me a unique perspective and skill set that I can bring to the field of webDev. I have the right combination of skills, experience and a willingness to learn and adapt.
In 2023 I graduated from Link Academy School and got my certificate as a fully fledged JavaScript Frontend Developer. It was a hard task, but with great effort and endurance, I have managed to achieve my dream, that of becoming a web developer. As a Web developer I use a combination of programming languages, software tools, and frameworks to build websites and web applications. I am proficient in some of the most common programming languages used in web development include HTML, CSS, JavaScript, NodeJS and PHP. For the past year I have working a freelancer on some of the most popular platforms, such as Upwork and Fiverr. As a freelancer, I have honed my skills in developing innovative web solutions that meet the needs of both the user and the business. My expertise in JavaScript ES6/TypeScript, HTML5, CSS3, jQuery, bootstrap5, React JS and Vue JS frameworks, as well as having experience with WordPress, have allowed me to deliver high-quality, interactive websites that engage and inform visitors. I have also been responsible for troubleshooting and debugging issues, as well as conducting code reviews to ensure that all development adheres to industry best practices.
