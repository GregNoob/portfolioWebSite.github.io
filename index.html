About

Hi!
My name is Caplea George Alexandru and for about a year now, I have dedicated myself to web designing and all things that exist in the world of front-end development. In february 2022 I've enrolled at Link Academy School, the web developemnt section and during 12 months I have been learning JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and all the basic concepts of web development, as well as popular frameworks such as React, bootstrap, tailwind and others. My educational background is different form anything that comes close to programming. Even so, I have gained key values and people skills that will serve my well in my future career as a web developer.
In 2014 I graduated from Law School at the University of Craiova and I went on to earn a master's degree as well, from the same University. I got my master's degree in 2016, and from then up until 2021, I held the position of Legal Advisor at two of the most important compaines in the food and turism industry in Romania. I have worked with all kinds of people over the years, from junior to senior exectives, from the lowest to the highest ranking posisons in corporate world. It was during that time that I've developed my social and work skills, as a profesional and as a team player. Skills that I now have the opportunity to put into practice as a junior web developer.
